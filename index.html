Need of Algorithm

Main purposes of CPU Scheduling Algorithm which is listed below

CPU Utilization

CPU utilization refers to the amount of time the central processing unit (CPU) spends executing processes and performing tasks. It is a critical performance metric in computing systems and is often used to assess the efficiency of the CPU's utilization of available processing power.

Performance

CPU performance refers to the efficiency and capability of a central processing unit (CPU) to execute instructions and perform computations within a computer system. It is a critical factor in determining the overall speed and responsiveness of a computing device. CPU performance is influenced by various factors, including architecture, clock speed, number of cores, cache size, and underlying microarchitecture.

Time Management

The goal is to maximize overall system performance and ensure fair and timely execution of tasks. Various scheduling algorithms are employed to manage the allocation of CPU time to processes.
